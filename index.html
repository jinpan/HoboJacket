The fine text

HoboJacket is meant to be an edgy way to incentivize more people to donate. Made with love by MIT students procrastinating on their homework, we hope you find our service tastefully offensive, laugh, and help out the unfortunate with Caltech apparel. As a not-for-profit site, we will make sure all donations go towards warming the homeless. Jacket quantities are computed assuming that we can purchase jackets at $10 apiece and ignoring all transactional fees and taxes. While we strive to purchase economically, we cannot guarantee that we will match our target price point. Contact us at hobojacket@mit.edu.
